Welcome

Tracker for CS:GO is a simple but feature-full application that tries to help you improve your game. Featuring match statistics, smoke tutorials and much more, this app allows you to train even when not behind PC.

Screenshots :

http://imgur.com/a/lSR9V

Highlights

• A beautiful rich Material design user interface with customizable options

• Night theme to save your eyes during night

• Save your matches

• Track statistics, see your progress

• See steam profile stats of you, or your friends. (public profile required)

• Learn common smoke and nade spots! Easy to find mid-game when you need just the one to ruin the day of enemy sniper

• Check out current streamers on twitch, streams just one click away

• Match ticker powered by http://www.gosugamers.net/ - see live matches, upcoming and latest results on one screen
